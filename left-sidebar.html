Services

AI • Cybersecurity • Data • Enterprise • App Development

Shree Raajah helps businesses build secure and scalable technology solutions. Whether you’re launching a new product, improving your security posture, modernizing systems, or turning data into decisions — we deliver with clear milestones, clean engineering, and long-term maintainability.

Our approach is simple: understand your goals, design the right architecture, build securely, launch reliably, and keep improving. We work with startups and enterprises and can support you as a full delivery partner or as an expert team augmenting your staff.

AI Solutions

We build practical AI systems that help teams save time and improve decision-making. From LLM chatbots to workflow automation, we focus on measurable outcomes and safe deployment.

Typical work includes: LLM chatbots, RAG/knowledge search, document intelligence, automation agents, custom model integration, evaluation and monitoring, and AI safety guardrails.

Cybersecurity

We reduce risk without slowing down your teams. Security is integrated into architecture and delivery — not added at the end.

Typical work includes: security assessments, secure architecture review, IAM/SSO, cloud security controls, vulnerability remediation support, secure CI/CD practices, logging/monitoring, and compliance-ready engineering.

Data Analytics

We help you build reliable data pipelines and dashboards so stakeholders get clear KPIs and real-time insights.

Typical work includes: ETL/ELT pipelines, data modeling, BI dashboards, KPI tracking, forecasting, anomaly detection, and analytics foundations for AI initiatives.

Enterprise Technology

Modernize and integrate systems for reliability, performance, and scale. We help reduce operational friction and simplify platforms.

Typical work includes: cloud migration, API platforms, microservices, integrations, legacy modernization, automation, DevOps, and enterprise application extensions.

App Development

End-to-end web and mobile development with clean UI, scalable backends, strong testing practices, and production deployment.

Typical work includes: product MVP builds, responsive web apps, mobile apps, backend APIs, databases, authentication, performance optimization, and post-launch support.
